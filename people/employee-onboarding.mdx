The Employee Onboarding Portal guides you through completing required employment paperwork. All documents are signed electronically and stored securely.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

<Check>Your login credentials from HR</Check>
<Check>Your Social Security Number (for tax forms)</Check>
<Check>Bank account information (for direct deposit)</Check>

## Accessing the Portal

1. Check your email for an invitation from Saguaro Transport
2. Click the confirmation link in the email
3. You'll be redirected to the onboarding portal at `/onboarding`

<Note>
If you haven't received an invitation email, contact HR at **(602) 905-5116** or email **hr@saguarotransport.com**
</Note>

## Portal Navigation

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Home** | Welcome page with progress overview |
| **Checklist** | Interactive 5-step onboarding checklist |
| **Documents** | Electronic document signing |
| **Resources** | Training materials and policies |
| **Team** | Directory of key contacts |

## Onboarding Steps

Complete each step in order to finish your onboarding:

### Step 1: Sign Documents

Review and sign initial employment documents:
- Employment agreement
- Company policies acknowledgment
- Confidentiality agreement

### Step 2: Code of Ethics

Read and acknowledge the company Code of Ethics covering:
- Professional conduct standards
- Ethical business practices
- Reporting procedures

### Step 3: W-9 Form

Complete the IRS W-9 form for tax purposes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Legal Name | Your full legal name |
| Tax Classification | Individual, LLC, Corporation, etc. |
| Address | Your current mailing address |
| SSN/EIN | Social Security or Employer ID Number |

### Step 4: Tax Withholding (W-4)

Set up your federal tax withholding:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Filing Status | Single, Married, Head of Household |
| Dependents | Number of qualifying dependents |
| Additional Withholding | Extra amount to withhold per paycheck |

### Step 5: Direct Deposit

Configure your payroll direct deposit:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Bank Name | Name of your financial institution |
| Routing Number | 9-digit bank routing number |
| Account Number | Your bank account number |
| Account Type | Checking or Savings |

## Signing Documents

All documents use electronic signatures:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Document">
    Click **Sign & Submit** on the document card
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Form">
    Complete all required fields (marked with *)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draw Signature">
    Use your mouse, trackpad, or finger to sign in the signature box
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit Document** to finalize
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you make a mistake on your signature, click **Clear** to start over before submitting.
</Tip>

## Document Statuses

After submission, your documents go through review:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Submitted** | Received, pending HR review |
| **Approved** | HR has verified and approved |
| **Rejected** | Needs revision (you'll be notified) |

## Completing Onboarding

Once you submit the Direct Deposit form (Step 5):
- Your status automatically changes to **Active**
- You gain access to the **Driver Portal**
- HR will review your documents and reach out if needed

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't access the onboarding portal">
    - Verify you're logged in with the correct email
    - Contact HR to confirm your employee record is set up
    - Ask HR to resend your invitation email
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My signature isn't saving">
    - Try using a mouse instead of trackpad
    - On mobile, draw directly on the signature area
    - Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
    - Clear browser cache and reload the page
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My document was rejected">
    - Check the Documents tab for the rejection reason
    - Contact HR for clarification if needed
    - Resubmit with the requested corrections
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Form fields won't submit">
    - Check all fields marked with asterisks (*)
    - Ensure SSN format is XXX-XX-XXXX
    - Routing number must be exactly 9 digits
    - Account number should not contain special characters
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
