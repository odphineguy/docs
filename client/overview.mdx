## Client Portal

The Client Portal gives you visibility into all your shipments with Saguaro Transport.

## Key Features

### Shipment Tracking
Track your deliveries in real-time:
- Live location of your shipment
- Estimated time of arrival
- Status updates at each milestone
- Driver information

### Invoice Management
View and manage your billing:
- Access all invoices
- Download PDF copies
- View payment history
- Track outstanding balances

### Proof of Delivery
Access delivery confirmation:
- Recipient signatures
- Delivery photos
- Timestamps and GPS data
- Download for your records

### Rate Quotes
Get pricing for new shipments:
- Enter origin and destination
- Select service type
- Receive instant quotes
- Book directly from quote

## Getting Started

1. Log in with your credentials
2. View your dashboard for an overview
3. Click any shipment to see details
4. Use the menu to access invoices and quotes
