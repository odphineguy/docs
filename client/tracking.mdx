## Tracking Your Shipments

### From the Dashboard
Your dashboard shows all active shipments with current status. Click any shipment for detailed tracking.

### By Tracking Number
Enter your tracking number in the search bar to find a specific shipment.

## Shipment Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Scheduled | Pickup scheduled but not yet started |
| Dispatched | Driver assigned and en route to pickup |
| Picked Up | Goods collected, in transit |
| In Transit | On the way to destination |
| Out for Delivery | Driver approaching destination |
| Delivered | Successfully delivered |

## Real-Time Updates

The tracking page shows:
- **Live Map** - Current vehicle location
- **ETA** - Estimated arrival time
- **Timeline** - All status updates with timestamps
- **Driver Info** - Name and contact (when applicable)

## Notifications

Set up alerts to receive:
- Email notifications at each milestone
- SMS alerts for delivery
- Delay notifications
