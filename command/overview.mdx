## Command Center

The Command Center is the operations hub for dispatchers and fleet managers. From here you can monitor your entire fleet in real-time, assign tasks, and communicate with drivers.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard.png" alt="Command Center Dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Key Features

### Real-Time Fleet Map
View all vehicles on a live map with:
- Current location and heading
- Driver status (active, idle, offline)
- Task assignments and routes

### Task Management
Create, assign, and track delivery tasks:
- Set pickup and delivery locations
- Assign priority levels
- Add special instructions
- Track status from created to delivered

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/workorders.png" alt="Workorders Panel" />
</Frame>

### Driver Communication
Stay connected with your drivers:
- One-on-one messaging
- Broadcast messages to all drivers
- System alerts and notifications

  <img src="/images/broadcast.png" alt="Broadcast Message" />

### Analytics & Reporting
Monitor operations performance:
- Delivery completion rates
- On-time performance
- Driver utilization
- Route efficiency
