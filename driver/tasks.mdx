## Viewing Your Tasks

Your task list shows all assigned deliveries. Each task displays:

- **Task ID** - Unique identifier
- **Type** - Freight, Documents, RX, etc.
- **Priority** - Color-coded by urgency
- **Addresses** - Pickup and delivery locations
- **Time Window** - When delivery is expected

## Task Workflow

### 1. Accept Task
When assigned a new task, review the details and tap **Accept** to confirm.

### 2. Start Route
Tap **Start** to begin navigation to the pickup location. The app will provide turn-by-turn directions.

### 3. Arrive at Pickup
When you arrive, tap **Arrived** to notify dispatch. Load the cargo and verify details.

### 4. Depart for Delivery
Tap **Picked Up** to mark cargo loaded. Navigate to the delivery address.

### 5. Complete Delivery
At the destination:
1. Tap **Arrived at Destination**
2. Deliver the goods
3. Capture proof of delivery
4. Tap **Complete**

## Handling Issues

If you encounter problems:
- Use the **Report Issue** button
- Message dispatch directly
- Document with photos if needed
