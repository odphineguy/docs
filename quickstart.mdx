## Accessing the Platform

Saguaro Transport has different portals depending on your role:

### For Dispatchers & Operations

Access the **Command Center** at your organization's Saguaro URL to:
- Monitor fleet locations in real-time
- Create and assign tasks to drivers
- Communicate with drivers via messaging
- Track deliveries and view audit trails

<Card title="Command Center Guide" icon="tower-broadcast" href="/command/overview">
  Learn how to use the Command Center
</Card>

### For Drivers

Access the **Driver Portal** to:
- View your assigned tasks
- Get optimized routes
- Capture proof of delivery (signatures, photos, barcodes)
- Communicate with dispatch

<Card title="Driver Portal Guide" icon="truck" href="/driver/overview">
  Learn how to use the Driver Portal
</Card>

### For Clients

Access the **Client Portal** to:
- Track your shipments in real-time
- View and download invoices
- Access visual proof of delivery
- Request rate quotes
